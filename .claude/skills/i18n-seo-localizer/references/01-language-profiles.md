# Language Profiles for SEO Localization

## Japanese (ja) - 日本語

### Market Overview
- **Traffic share**: 4.2% of global AI image generator traffic
- **Payment intent**: Very high (strong purchasing culture)
- **Cultural context**: Anime/manga culture, illustration-focused

### SEO Specifications

| Element | English | Japanese | Notes |
|---------|---------|----------|-------|
| Title | 50-60 chars | 25-35 chars | Japanese chars = ~2 bytes |
| Description | 140-160 chars | 70-100 chars | Shorter but information-dense |
| H1 | Contains primary keyword | Contains primary keyword | Single H1 rule same |

### Keyword Preferences

| English Concept | Preferred Japanese | Avoid |
|-----------------|-------------------|-------|
| AI image generator | AI イラスト生成 | AI画像生成器 (too technical) |
| free | 無料 | フリー (less common for tools) |
| online | オンライン | ネット上で |
| easy to use | 簡単操作 | 使いやすい (less punchy) |

### Cultural Considerations
- **イラスト vs 画像**: Use イラスト for creative/artistic contexts
- **Formality**: Use です/ます form for UI and marketing copy
- **Visual style**: Anime/illustration style resonates better than photorealistic

### Top Competitors (Japan)
1. Canva Japan (canva.com/ja_jp)
2. MyEdit (myedit.online/jp)
3. Novel AI (novelai.net)
4. Adobe Firefly Japan

### Google Trends Validation URL Template
```
https://trends.google.com/trends/explore?geo=JP&q={term1},{term2},{term3}
```

## Korean (ko) - 한국어

### Market Overview
- **Traffic share**: 8.3% of global AI image generator traffic (2nd largest!)
- **Payment intent**: High
- **Cultural context**: Tech-savvy users, K-pop/K-drama aesthetic preferences

### SEO Specifications

| Element | English | Korean | Notes |
|---------|---------|--------|-------|
| Title | 50-60 chars | 25-40 chars | Hangul efficient |
| Description | 140-160 chars | 70-110 chars | Slightly longer than Japanese |
| H1 | Contains primary keyword | Contains primary keyword | Same rules |

### Keyword Preferences

| English Concept | Preferred Korean | Avoid |
|-----------------|-----------------|-------|
| AI image generator | AI 이미지 생성기 | AI 화상 생성기 |
| AI drawing | 그림 그려주는 AI | AI 드로잉 (less searched) |
| free | 무료 | 공짜 (too casual) |
| online | 온라인 | 인터넷상에서 |

### Cultural Considerations
- **English loanwords**: Koreans are comfortable with English terms (AI, 온라인, etc.)
- **Formality**: Use 합니다/합니까 for professional tone
- **Visual style**: Modern, polished K-beauty/K-pop aesthetics preferred

### Top Competitors (Korea)
1. Carat AI (carat.im)
2. MyEdit Korea
3. Midjourney (via Discord)
4. WRTN (뤼튼)

### Google Trends Validation URL Template
```
https://trends.google.com/trends/explore?geo=KR&q={term1},{term2},{term3}
```

## German (de) - Deutsch

### Market Overview
- **Traffic share**: ~4% of global traffic
- **Payment intent**: Very high (strongest in Europe)
- **Cultural context**: Privacy-conscious, quality-focused

### SEO Specifications

| Element | English | German | Notes |
|---------|---------|--------|-------|
| Title | 50-60 chars | 55-65 chars | German ~30% longer |
| Description | 140-160 chars | 150-170 chars | Account for compound words |
| H1 | Contains primary keyword | Contains primary keyword | Same rules |

### Keyword Preferences

| English Concept | Preferred German | Avoid |
|-----------------|-----------------|-------|
| AI image generator | KI Bilder erstellen | KI-Bildgenerator (less natural) |
| free | kostenlos | gratis (less professional) |
| online | online | im Internet |
| easy | einfach | simpel |

### Cultural Considerations
- **KI vs AI**: Germans prefer "KI" (Künstliche Intelligenz) over "AI"
- **Compound words**: German creates long compound words (Bildgenerierungssoftware)
- **Privacy**: Mention data protection/GDPR compliance

### Google Trends Validation URL Template
```
https://trends.google.com/trends/explore?geo=DE&q={term1},{term2},{term3}
```

## Spanish (es) - Español

### Market Overview
- **Traffic share**: Significant (LATAM + Spain combined)
- **Payment intent**: Medium (varies by country)
- **Cultural context**: Diverse market across 20+ countries

### SEO Specifications

| Element | English | Spanish | Notes |
|---------|---------|---------|-------|
| Title | 50-60 chars | 55-65 chars | Similar to English |
| Description | 140-160 chars | 145-165 chars | Slightly longer |

### Regional Variations

| Concept | Spain (ES) | Mexico (MX) | Argentina (AR) |
|---------|-----------|-------------|----------------|
| Computer | ordenador | computadora | computadora |
| Free | gratis | gratis | gratis |
| Create | crear | crear | crear |

### Keyword Preferences

| English Concept | Preferred Spanish | Notes |
|-----------------|------------------|-------|
| AI image generator | generador de imágenes IA | Word order matters |
| create images with AI | crear imágenes con IA | Natural phrasing |
| free | gratis | Universal across regions |

### Google Trends Validation URL Template
```
https://trends.google.com/trends/explore?geo=ES&q={term1},{term2}
https://trends.google.com/trends/explore?geo=MX&q={term1},{term2}
```

- [ ] Confirmed cultural appropriateness
